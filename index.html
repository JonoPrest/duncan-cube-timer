Using the timer

To use the timer, simply hold down the space bar until the time in the middle of the screen turns green. At that point, upon releasing the space bar, the timer will start, and won't stop again until a key is pressed, at which point it will stop and log your time.

In addition to timing your solves and calculating averages and statistics, this timer also generates scrambles for each solve, which can be seen at the top of the screen. You are welcome to use these scrambles, which are stored along with each time, or hand scramble the cube yourself.

The timer supports the storage of multiple sets of times as well. You can select a different event at the top of the screen, which includes most common WCA events as well as an "Other" category. It does not currently support the creation of custom events.
